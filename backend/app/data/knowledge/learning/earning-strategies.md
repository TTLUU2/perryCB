# Best Strategies for Earning Points in Australia

Building a large points balance does not require flying constantly. Most Australians earn the bulk of their points on the ground through smart financial products and everyday spending habits.

## Credit Card Sign-Up Bonuses

This is the single fastest way to earn points. Many Australian credit cards offer sign-up bonuses ranging from 60,000 to 200,000+ points when you meet a minimum spend requirement (typically $3,000–$6,000 within the first three months). A well-timed card application can earn you enough points for a business class flight in one go. Always check the annual fee and ongoing earn rate before applying.

## Everyday Spending

Once you have a points-earning credit card, channel as much of your regular spending through it as possible — groceries, bills, insurance, and subscriptions. Cards with strong earn rates offer 0.75–1.5 points per dollar on general spending and up to 3 points per dollar in bonus categories. Pay your balance in full each month to avoid interest charges eating into your points value.

## Transfer Partners

Programs like American Express Membership Rewards and Citi Rewards allow you to transfer points to multiple airline programs. This gives you flexibility — you can wait until you know where you want to fly, then transfer to whichever program offers the best redemption.

## Shopping Portals

The Qantas Shopping portal and Velocity eStore offer bonus points at hundreds of online retailers. You can earn 2–10 points per dollar at stores you already shop at, simply by clicking through the portal first.

## Dining and Fuel Programs

Qantas Dining Rewards lets you earn points at participating restaurants. On the fuel side, earning Qantas Points at BP or Velocity points at Shell adds up over time, particularly for frequent drivers.

## Stacking Strategies

The real power comes from combining methods. For example, you could shop through the Qantas Shopping portal, pay with a Qantas-earning credit card, and scan your Qantas membership at checkout — earning points three times on a single purchase.
