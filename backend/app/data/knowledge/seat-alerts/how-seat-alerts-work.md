# How Point Hacks Seat Alerts Work

Reward seat availability is one of the biggest challenges in the points game. Airlines release a limited number of seats that can be booked with points, and these seats appear and disappear unpredictably. Point Hacks Seat Alerts solves this problem by continuously monitoring award availability and notifying you the moment seats open up.

## What Seat Alerts Does

You tell us the route you want to fly, your preferred travel dates (or a date range), the cabin class, and which frequent flyer program you plan to use. Our system then monitors airline inventory around the clock, checking for reward seat availability on your behalf.

## How Monitoring Works

Our automated system queries airline reward seat databases at regular intervals. When a matching seat appears — whether it is a newly released seat or a cancellation that has freed up space — you receive a notification immediately so you can book before someone else snaps it up.

## Notifications

Alerts are delivered via email and, for Premium subscribers, also via push notifications. Each alert includes the route, dates, cabin class, points cost, and a direct link to help you book quickly.

## Supported Airlines and Routes

Seat Alerts covers major programs relevant to Australian travellers, including:

- **Qantas** Classic Flight Rewards (domestic and international)
- **Velocity** reward seats on Virgin Australia and partner airlines
- **Singapore Airlines / KrisFlyer** award seats in business and first class
- Popular routes from **Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, and Adelaide** to key international destinations

Whether you are hunting for Qantas business class to London or Singapore Airlines Suites from Sydney, Seat Alerts monitors the availability so you do not have to manually check every day.
