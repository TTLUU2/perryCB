# Free vs Premium Seat Alerts

Point Hacks offers two tiers of Seat Alerts to suit different needs.

## Free Tier

- **Monitoring frequency:** Checked once per day.
- **Number of routes:** Monitor up to 2 active routes at a time.
- **Notifications:** Email alerts only.
- **Notification speed:** Alerts sent within 24 hours of availability being detected.
- **Price:** Free for all Point Hacks members.

The free tier is ideal if you are flexible on dates and not in a rush to book — for example, planning a trip six months or more in advance.

## Premium Tier

- **Monitoring frequency:** Checked multiple times per hour for near-real-time detection.
- **Number of routes:** Monitor up to 10 active routes simultaneously.
- **Notifications:** Email alerts plus instant push notifications.
- **Notification speed:** Alerts delivered within minutes of availability appearing.
- **Additional features:** Priority support, historical availability trends, and the ability to set complex date ranges.
- **Price:** Paid monthly or annual subscription (check the Point Hacks website for current pricing).

## Which Tier Should You Choose?

If you are chasing highly competitive routes — such as Qantas first class to London, Singapore Airlines Suites, or peak-season business class to Tokyo — **Premium** is strongly recommended. These seats can appear and disappear within hours, and the faster notification speed makes a significant difference.

For less competitive routes or flexible travel plans, the **Free** tier is a great starting point and costs you nothing.
