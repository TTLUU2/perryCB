# Buying Qantas &amp; Velocity Points

**Buying Qantas & Velocity Points**

Buying points from Qantas or Velocity Frequent Flyer is not quite as lucrative as it is with certain overseas airlines. But if you find yourself short, it is possible to buy small amounts of Qantas and Velocity points. This unit explains how.

In this training unit, we will cover...

## Velocity Points Booster

If you need more Velocity points fast, you can purchase up to 250,000 Velocity points per calendar year using Velocity Points Booster.

Need more Velocity points to reach your next reward? You can buy them. Photo: Virgin Australia.

Points can be purchased in pre-determined increments starting at 1,000 points. There is no limit to the number of transactions you can make.

To be eligible to buy points, you must already have at least one Velocity point in your account.

Velocity Points Booster purchases are quite expensive, so we don’t generally recommend buying bulk amounts. But if you're just a few thousand points short of a specific reward, it could be worthwhile. It could also be worth buying points during a bonus or discount offer, if you have a specific redemption in mind.

## Velocity Points Booster discounts and bonuses

Velocity Frequent Flyer typically offers bonus points or a discount on Velocity Points Booster purchases up to a few times per year.

The best ever promotion was 100% bonus points in October 2020. That particular offer hasn't been repeated since, but we have seen discounts of 10-40% available.

Offers are typically tiered, with a higher discount or bonus available if you buy more points.

## Qantas top-up points

Qantas similarly sells top-up points at rather expensive prices. You can buy between 1,000 and 150,000 Qantas points at a time, up to four times per year. Prices start at $56 for 1,000 points, up to $3,999 for 150,000 Qantas points.

Qantas sells top-up points. Photo: Matt Graham.

To purchase top-up points, you will need to have been a Qantas Frequent Flyer member for at least 30 days. You can purchase points on the Qantas website.

Screenshot from the Qantas top-up points webpage.

It is also possible to buy points by calling the Qantas Frequent Flyer Service Centre on 13 11 31 (from within Australia), but there is a $35 call centre fee. More information is available on the Qantas website.

## Bonus point offers on Qantas top-up points

Qantas Frequent Flyer typically offers 20-60% bonus points on top-up points purchases a few times per year during limited-time promotions.

Even with these offers, buying top-up points is not the best value way to get extra Qantas points. But it is at least a fast way to get more points if you need some urgently.

## Qantas Wine deals

Another method of purchasing Qantas points is with bonus point offers from Qantas Wine. Qantas' online wine retailer regularly offers very generous amounts of bonus points on wine purchases. You can see current offers on the Qantas Wine website.

Earn 24,000 bonus Qantas points with this deal. Screenshot from the Qantas Wine website.

This method is usually cheaper than buying top-up points... and you get to enjoy some wine as well!

In the example above (a promotion from November 2023), you'd get 24,000 bonus Qantas points with a case of wine costing $480. It would cost $747 to buy the same amount of top-up points.

The Qantas points generally post within 1-2 days of your order being delivered, though may take a bit longer at times.

## Family transfers

Need a few more points quickly? Rather than buying them, you could see if a family member is willing to "lend" you their points.

Both Qantas and Velocity Frequent Flyer allow transfers between family members' accounts for free:

- With Qantas Frequent Flyer, you can transfer an unlimited amount of points to/from eligible family members with a minimum of 1,500 points per transfer

- With Velocity, you can transfer points to/from an eligible family member up to four times per year. Transfers must be between 5,000-125,000 points.
