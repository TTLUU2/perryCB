## **Are you a travel & frequent flyer program expert?**

Courses 3-10 of AFF's Frequent Flyer Training are expert courses, which are available exclusively to AFF Platinum members. These courses are packed full of helpful tips and strategies to help you maximise your frequent flyer points, status and travel experience!

Here's a brief overview of what you can learn in AFF's expert courses:

- Course 3: Transferring & Redeeming Points explains the best ways to redeem your points with Qantas, Velocity and other loyalty programs

- Course 4: The Road to Platinum Status shows the shortcuts to earning elite airline status

- Course 5: Finding & Booking Reward Flights explains how to easily find reward flight availability

- Course 6: Buying Points explains how to buy points for cheap premium cabin flights

- Course 7: Everybody Loves an Upgrade covers the many ways of upgrading flights

- Course 8: Cheap Airfares & Round-the-World Tickets will help you to save thousands of dollars on flights

- Course 9: Hotel, Supermarket & Car Hire Loyalty Programs explains how to get value from loyalty programs on the ground

- Course 10: Travel Hacks covers lots of tricks to help you plan a great trip while saving lots of money.
