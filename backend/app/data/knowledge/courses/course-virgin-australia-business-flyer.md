# Virgin Australia Business Flyer

**Virgin Australia Business Flyer**

Fast-track your way to Velocity Gold status and save money on flights & lounge membership by joining the free Virgin Australia Business Flyer program for small to medium-sized businesses.

In this training unit, we will cover…

## What is Virgin Australia Business Flyer?

The corporate travel market is a lucrative one, so it's no surprise that Virgin Australia offers a loyalty program of its own for small to medium-sized businesses spending up to $300,000 per year on air travel.

Until April 2022, Virgin Australia offered a program for businesses called Accelerate. That program didn't award points or have any membership tiers. The new Virgin Australia Business Flyer program that replaced Accelerate is much more similar to Qantas Business Rewards.

Virgin Australia Business Flyer offers various discounts and benefits to businesses with employees that fly Virgin Australia. Businesses can also earn points when employees fly with Qatar Airways, Singapore Airlines or United Airlines.

Here is a summary of the main benefits:

- Earn Velocity Points for the business (in addition to the Velocity Points earned by the flyer)

- Access to 2x Pilot Gold memberships

- Fare discounts

- Discounted annual Virgin Australia Lounge membership

- Exclusive offers

**Virgin Australia Business Flyer is completely free to join for businesses with an active ABN or ACN and an annual spend on flights of up to $300,000. There are no ongoing fees**. However, according to the program terms & conditions:

Please note that Superannuation Funds, travel agents, travel management companies, travel wholesalers, air travel consolidators, or anyone re-selling air travel are not eligible to join the VA Business Flyer program.

You can sign up on the Virgin Australia website.

## Flight discounts

All Virgin Australia Business Flyer members can access the following "Fare advantage discounts" on eligible Virgin Australia flights when booking through the Virgin Australia Business Flyer booking portal, Guest Contact Centre or with a registered travel agent:

- Up to 4% discount off Virgin Australia Economy Flex fares

- Up to 6% discount off Virgin Australia Business fares

- 15-20% discount off United Airlines Economy Flex and Business fares booked as a "VA" codeshare

The discount amount depends on the route and class of travel and does not apply to taxes. See the Virgin Australia website for more information.

## Membership tiers

Virgin Australia Business Flyer has 3 membership tiers. Your business' level is determined by the amount spent on flights during the past year.

At the moment, the only real additional benefit of Tier 2 and Tier 3 membership seems to be a higher earn rate of Velocity points for the business. The Virgin Australia website says that there are also other "additional benefits" for Tier 2 & 3 members.

## Velocity Pilot Gold membership

Perhaps the biggest drawcard of the Virgin Australia Business Flyer program is the shortcut to Velocity Gold status. All businesses signed up to the program can nominate up to two employees to receive a Pilot Gold membership by spending at least $2,000 on Virgin Australia flights within the first 3 months of joining.

Both Pilot Gold members then receive 3 months of complimentary Velocity Gold trial status. Each flyer can upgrade to full Velocity Gold status for another year by earning at least 80 status credits and one eligible VA sector within the trial period. (This is well short of the 500 status credits and 4 eligible flights normally required to earn Gold status!)

As an example, a one-way Virgin Australia Business flight from Melbourne to Brisbane would be enough to earn those 80 status credits and 1 eligible sector. (See the status credit table on the Velocity website for earn rates on other routes and classes of travel.)

During the 3-month Pilot Gold membership, nominated flyers receive most of the usual benefits of Velocity Gold status including lounge access, bonus points, priority check-in and priority boarding.
