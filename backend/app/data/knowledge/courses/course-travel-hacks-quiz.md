# Travel Hacks Quiz

## **Congratulations for completing the Australian Frequent Flyer training!**

Congratulations! You've almost reached the end of the training program. We hope that you've learnt a lot over the last 10 training courses, and that you'll put many of the tips, tricks and strategies to good use. If you do, we're confident you'll be able to travel further, more often and in greater comfort!

Your frequent flyer education doesn't end here! Feel free to go back and read up on anything you missed. We regularly update the training, so you can also refer back to this training as things change.
