## Get lounge access with the right credit card

Credit cards aren't *just *great for earning lots of frequent flyer points! Many also come with complimentary airport lounge access. This could be in the form of some single-use lounge passes, or even a membership with unlimited lounge visits.

In fact, in some cases, paying the credit card's annual fee is more cost-effective than buying a lounge membership!

This final training unit for Course 2 includes a list of Australian credit cards with airport lounge benefits.

## Amex Centurion Lounge access

Many Australian-issued American Express cards come with complimentary visits each calendar year to the Amex Centurion Lounges in Sydney and Melbourne. These lounges are both located in the international terminals at these airports, and are accessible regardless of which airline you're flying.

The Amex Centurion Lounge at Sydney Airport. Photo: American Express.

Additional visits above the included allowance (and extra guests) are charged at $55 each.

See below for a list of cards that include Amex Centurion Lounge access.

## Cards offering unlimited global lounge network access

The following credit cards offer either a large number of visits or unlimited access to partner airport lounges for the cardholder. In some cases, complimentary lounge access is also available to one guest with each visit.

With the MyCard Prestige card, you can even use your included lounge visits as dining credits (with a same-day boarding pass) at airport cafés, bars and restaurants that partner with Priority Pass. Note that complimentary Priority Pass memberships received via an American Express card do NOT come with these airport dining credits.
