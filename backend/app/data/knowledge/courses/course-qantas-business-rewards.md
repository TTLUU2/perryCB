# Qantas Business Rewards

**Qantas Business Rewards**

Earn extra Qantas Points and save money on Qantas flights by signing your business up to Qantas Business Rewards.

In this training unit, we will cover…

## What is Qantas Business Rewards?

Both Qantas and Virgin Australia have separate loyalty programs aimed at small-to-medium businesses. Qantas' is aptly called Qantas Business Rewards.

If you have a business with an Australian Business Number (ABN), you could be eligible to join.

As a Qantas Business Rewards member, your business can earn Qantas Points for flying with Qantas or spending with program partners. You can then redeem those points directly from your Business Rewards account, or transfer them at a 1:1 rate to any Qantas Frequent Flyer member.

The program also offers three tiers, based on your business' flying spend, which come with various discounts and extra benefits.

## How to join Qantas Business Rewards for free

Qantas Business Rewards normally has an $89.50 joining fee, but there is no need to pay this!

Qantas Business Rewards regularly offers promotions where you can sign up for free. Outside of these promotions, you can still join for free using a promotional link offered by one of the program's partners. Even if you're not a customer of that particular partner, you can still sign up for free using their "free join" link.

Any of the below links should allow you to join Qantas Business Rewards for free:

- NRMA "free join" link

- Red Energy "free join" link

There are no ongoing membership fees.

## How to earn points with Qantas Business Rewards

There are two main ways to earn points in your Qantas Business Rewards account...

## Earn by spending with program partners

There are dozens of earning partners aimed specifically at businesses. These include:

- Qantas Hotels

- Qantas Wine

- Qantas Business Money

- Harvey Norman Commercial

- Snap

- Deloitte

- Avis & Budget vehicle hire

- Live Payments

- Xero

- BP Plus

- Accor Plus (earn points when buying an Accor Plus Business Explorer membership)

You'll earn points for every dollar spent with program partners. See the Qantas Business Rewards website for a full list of partners and earn rates.

## Earn by flying

Your business will earn Qantas Points for any Qantas flight/s taken by employees linked to your business. You can also earn on Jetstar flights when purchased with a bundle that is normally eligible to earn Qantas Points.

These are earned as a percentage of the points earned by the person taking the flight, depending on your membership level:

- Level 1: 20% of points earned

- Level 2: 30% of points earned

- Level 3: 40% of points earned

Points are only earned on flights marketed by Qantas (i.e. with a "QF" flight number) or Jetstar. The ticket must be purchased in Australia and the business' ABN must be added to the booking to earn points.

In addition to their regular points and status credits, the traveller will also earn 250 bonus Qantas Points per booking.

Both the business and the employee travelling can earn Qantas Points. Photo: Qantas.

## Redeeming Qantas Business Rewards points

Once you've earned at least 3,000 points, you'll be able to transfer these to any Qantas Frequent Flyer account. This could be your own personal account, or the account of a friend, relative, employee or anyone else. Unlike regular transfers between Qantas Frequent Flyer accounts, the member does not need to be related to you.

Once converted, the points can be spent just like regular Qantas Points.

## Qantas Business Rewards points expiry

Like with Qantas Frequent Flyer, points in** Qantas Business Rewards expire if your account is inactive for 18 months**. Activity includes earning at least one point, redeeming points or transferring out your points.

## Flight discounts

If you or your employees fly often, there's one more advantage to joining up. Discounts are available on some airfare types.

To access this discount, you'll need to book through the Qantas Business Rewards portal or with a participating travel agent. The special member price will appear on the booking page. The discount amount will depend on your membership level.

A 20% discount on Qantas Club membership is also available to Level 1 Business Rewards members. This discount increases to 30% for Level 2 and 3 members.

## Membership levels

There are three membership levels in the Qantas Business Rewards program:

- **Level 1:** For businesses earning 0 - 14,999 points from flights per year

- **Level 2:** For businesses earning 15,000 - 79,999 points from flights per year

- **Level 3:** For businesses earning 80,000+ points from flights per year

Your business' membership level determines the amount of points you'll earn when employees fly with Qantas, as well as the discounts available on Qantas Club membership and eligible fares.

## Qantas Club membership discounts

As a Level 2 or 3 member, you can access a 30% discount off Qantas Club membership for employees. Level 1 members get a 20% discount.

## Qantas Gold status accelerator for Level 3 members

For each year that your business reaches Level 3, you'll also be able to nominate one traveller for a Gold Accelerator challenge.

With this challenge, the traveller can attain Qantas Frequent Flyer Gold status by earning at least 200 status credits from Qantas marketed and operated flights within 90 days.

Level 3 members can access a fast-track to Qantas Gold status.
