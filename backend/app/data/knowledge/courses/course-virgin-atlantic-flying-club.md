# Virgin Atlantic Flying Club

**Virgin Atlantic Flying Club**

Virgin Atlantic (not to be confused with Virgin Australia) is part of the SkyTeam alliance. You can buy its Virgin Points at a bonus and redeem across the SkyTeam network.

In this training unit, we will cover...

## Why buy Virgin Points?

So far, all of the frequent flyer programs we've looked at in this course belong to airlines that are part of Star Alliance, Oneworld or not affiliated with any alliance.

Virgin Atlantic, based in the UK, is a SkyTeam airline with a frequent flyer program that sells points with regular bonuses. This program opens up even more reward flight options on more airlines, through Virgin Atlantic's extensive network of SkyTeam and other partner airlines.

Through its SkyTeam membership, you can redeem Virgin Atlantic's "Virgin Points" on partner airlines including Garuda Indonesia, Delta, Vietnam Airlines and Kenya Airways.

Please note that Virgin Atlantic is a completely different airline to Virgin Australia. It has different partner airlines and uses Virgin Points as its currency, rather than Velocity points, and it is **not** possible to convert Virgin Points into Velocity points, or vice versa.

In addition to buying points, it's also possible to accumulate Virgin Points in Australia by transferring Amex Membership Rewards points to Virgin Atlantic Flying Club.

Virgin Points do not expire. You can even change your mind and get a refund within 14 days of buying Virgin Points, as long as you haven't redeemed any of the bought points.

## Buying Virgin Points

Virgin Atlantic Flying Club sells points for GBP15 (~AU$29) per thousand at full price, plus a transaction fee of GBP15 (~AU$29) for each purchase. (The transaction fee applies regardless of how many points you buy in each transaction.)

Virgin Atlantic runs promotions several times per year where you can also get up to 70% bonus points on purchases. These bonuses are often tiered, so that you get a larger bonus if you buy more points.

To buy Virgin Points, you **must already have at least one point in your Flying Club account**. Virgin Atlantic partners with American Express - this is the easiest way to get points in your Flying Club account. Otherwise, you can credit a flight to Flying Club or use an online shopping portal.

You can buy up to 100,000 points per year on the Virgin Atlantic website, excluding bonus points. Virgin Atlantic often doubles the annual purchase limit during promotions, so in practice, you could accumulate up to 340,000 points/year (including 70% bonus points when buying 200,000). Once purchased, the points should arrive in your account within 24 hours but may occasionally take longer.

## Award chart sweet spots

The first thing to know about Virgin Atlantic Flying Club redemptions is that they are priced on a per-flight basis. If you have more than one flight in your itinerary, you'll be charged the sum of every individual sector. So, this program is better for booking point-to-point flights than complicated multi-city itineraries. 

Virgin Atlantic uses dynamic reward seat pricing on its own flights. When redeeming on partner airlines, there a few different reward charts, depending on the airline flown. These are mostly distance-based charts.

There are specific reward charts with different prices listed for flights on Virgin Atlantic, Delta, KLM, Air France and all non-SkyTeam airlines.

## Award routing rules

As reward flights are priced based on the sum of individual sectors, award routing rules aren't all that relevant to Flying Club redemptions. Fly more, pay more points - it's as simple as that.

You can combine multiple flight sectors onto a single ticket, but you can't combine flights with different airlines onto the same one-way booking.

## Fees on Virgin Atlantic Flying Club award bookings

There is a GBP30 (~AU$58) fee for changes and cancellations. Cancellations are possible up to 24 hours before departure.

## Summary: Buying Virgin Points

- Virgin Atlantic Flying Club points cost GBP15 per thousand, plus a GBP15 processing fee per transaction

- Promotions are semi-frequent and typical offers are up to 70% bonus Virgin Points

- You can redeem Virgin Points for point-to-point flights on SkyTeam airlines and various other niche partner airlines
