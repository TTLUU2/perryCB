# Virgin Australia Upgrades

## Upgrading with Velocity points

Virgin Australia offers the following types of upgrades:

- **UpgradeMe Points** (upgrades with Velocity points)

- **UpgradeMe Platinum** (complimentary upgrades for Velocity Platinum members)

- **UpgradeMe Premium Bid** (bid upgrades)

In this unit, we'll look at the first two upgrade types. We'll cover bid upgrades in a later unit of this course.

You can use Velocity points for an UpgradeMe Points upgrade to Business Class on Virgin Australia flights. Photo: Virgin Australia.

**UpgradeMe Points** upgrades are available on any Virgin Australia domestic or international flight. They can be confirmed instantly if a Reward Seat is available on that flight in Business Class at the time of requesting the upgrade.

So if you're planning to request an upgrade with Velocity points,** just make sure you're booking a Virgin Australia flight with Business Reward availability**.

This flight has Business Reward availability. If you were eligible to upgrade on this flight, you could buy an upgradeable ticket and your request would be confirmed instantly.

You can also waitlist for an upgrade on flights with no Reward Seat availability, but there's no guarantee you'll be successful.

Velocity Frequent Flyer members can upgrade with points from Economy Choice and Economy Flex fares. **Economy Lite tickets and Velocity Reward Seat bookings cannot be upgraded**.

Upgrades can be requested online (in "Manage Booking") or via the Velocity Membership Contact Centre. You can also request a domestic upgrades at any Virgin Australia Lounge up to 30 minutes prior to departure. (Full in-flight catering is not assured on last-minute upgrades.)

It's not possible to upgrade using Velocity points on flights operated by partner airlines. (This was previously possible on some Delta flights for a short time.)

You can check the number of Velocity points required for an upgrade on the Velocity Frequent Flyer website. Note that different amounts of points are required to upgrade on domestic and international flights.

## Complimentary Platinum member upgrades

Velocity Platinum members receive four annual complimentary "UpgradeMe Platinum" upgrades. These upgrades can be used for the Platinum member and/or any travel companions on the same booking.

To use the UpgradeMe Platinum benefit as a Velocity Platinum member:

- You **must book an Economy Flex fare** to use the upgrade/s (except during special promotions where Economy Choice fares may be valid)

- There needs to be Business Reward availability on the flight

Velocity Platinum member upgrades come with some conditions. Photo: Virgin Australia.

If you wish to use your upgrade but there is no Reward Seat availability in Business class, you can call Velocity to request more seats to be released. This is not guaranteed to work but does sometimes. You're most likely to be successful if you call within one week before the flight (and there are still Business seats available for sale).

See the Velocity Frequent Flyer website for information about the UpgradeMe Platinum offer.
