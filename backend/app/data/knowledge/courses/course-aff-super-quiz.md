# AFF Super Quiz

**The AFF Super Quiz**

Take our AFF Super Quiz below to test your knowledge of airlines, frequent flyer programs, credit cards and airport lounges!

All AFF members get free access to the first two courses of our Frequent Flyer Training:

- Course 1: Introduction to Frequent Flyer Programs covers topics including airlines, alliances and frequent flyer programs, including earning points

- Course 2: Credit Cards & Airport Lounges covers topics including credit cards, business reward programs and airport lounges.

**How much do you know about these topics?** **Find out by taking our AFF Super Quiz below**!

**There are 30 multiple-choice questions. At the end of the quiz, you can see a breakdown of your score for the topics covered in each of our free courses. Good luck!** :)

After completing the quiz, feel free to share your results with the AFF community.

**This quiz is available for free to all AFF members. Simply log into your AFF account then click on the link below to get started!** **If you're not yet an AFF member, simply join here.**
