# ExpertFlyer

**ExpertFlyer**

ExpertFlyer is packed full of useful features for frequent flyers. One of these features is the ability to find award availability on many airlines, including those outside of the major alliances.

In this training unit, we will cover...

## How it works

ExpertFlyer works a little differently to other legacy tools such as the KVS Tool and Award Nexus. Instead of screen scraping the results of airline website searches, this paid subscription service gets its information from the Global Distribution System, or GDS.

The GDS is the system airlines use to publish industry information about their available airfares, generally for the use of travel agents. So the information that Expert Flyer provides is generally up-to-date, and accurate.

ExpertFlyer does have some limitations. As far as award availability is concerned, one of the main limitations is that it only provides information for a limited range of airlines. Many of the airlines in the three alliances are not actually covered. However, ExpertFlyer *does* cover a range of niche airlines outside of the major alliances that other tools do not.

If an airline only releases award availability to its own program and not to partners, Expert Flyer may not pick this up.

The ability to find award availability is not the only benefit of ExpertFlyer. The tool can also be used to find lots of other information including real-time flight loading information and seats maps, which could be useful if you're trying to secure an upgrade.

## Finding award availability with ExpertFlyer

To find award availability using Expert Flyer, click on the "Awards & Upgrades" tab. You can search for seats on one airline at a time, but you can search up to a week at a time by selecting "± 3 days".

You can conduct Award & Upgrade Availability searches using ExpertFlyer.

You'll find a video demonstration on using ExpertFlyer to find award availability below.

Please note that the award availability shown on ExpertFlyer generally reflects what is available to members of that airline's own frequent flyer program. This is often representative of what is also available to members of partner airline frequent flyer programs, but not always. For example, Qantas Frequent Flyer members generally have access to fewer reward seats on Qatar Airways flights than Qatar Airways' own Privilege Club members.

## Award seat alerts

If you're holding out for a reward seat on a specific flight, or just want to be notified if a seat becomes available, ExpertFlyer's email alerts could be very useful. You can set an alert and be notified if/when space opens up in the future!

Simply click on the alert icon next to any flight in the search results to set up the alert. Set and forget, and you'll get an email if seats become available. Premium subscribers can set up to 200 alerts at a time.

Click on the box with the exclamation mark to set up an award seat alert from the search results page on ExpertFlyer.

## Other uses for ExpertFlyer

Expert Flyer can help with more than just finding award availability! Other features of Expert Flyer include:

- Flight availability/loadings and flight alerts (useful when upgrading)

- Seat map searches and seat alerts

- Real-time flight status and reliability information

- Minimum Connect Time information

- Check visa requirements

- Set aircraft change alerts

- Search flight timetables

- Published fare information

- View interline agreements

- Check TSA security line wait times

- View possible connecting airports for travel between any city pair

## Subscription options

Expert Flyer works on a monthly subscription basis. **Basic subscriptions** cost USD4.99 per month. For this price you can make up to 250 searches per month.

A **Premium subscription** costs USD9.99 per month or USD99.99/year. For this price you can make unlimited searches. You also get access to flight alerts, and you can search for flights one week at a time, instead of only one day at a time. Our recommendation is to purchase the annual premium subscription for USD99.99 per year.

A full list of the benefits of each membership level can be found on the ExpertFlyer website.
