# SeatSpy

**SeatSpy**

With SeatSpy, you can view an entire year of reward seat availability on eligible airlines, for any route, all on one page.

In this training unit, we will cover...

## How SeatSpy works

SeatSpy lets you view an entire year worth of award availability on any given route on the following 13 airlines:

- Air France

- American Airlines

- Austrian

- British Airways

- Brussels Airlines

- Croatia Airlines

- Etihad Airways

- KLM

- LOT Polish Airlines

- Lufthansa

- Swiss

- United Airlines

- Virgin Atlantic

You can only search for award availability on one route at a time, and only on the airlines listed above. But if you want to fly with one of these airlines, the tool will instantly show you award availability on any route you search for each class of travel on a single screen, for all travel dates across the next year.

For example, you could use SeatSpy to search for British Airways reward seat availability from Singapore to London:

SeatSpy shows an overview of availability by month, with a full calendar below this. Screenshot from SeatSpy.com.

Below the Month Availability Overview, you’ll find a calendar showing availability on each individual date over the next year. This is colour-coded with each colour depicting a different cabin class.

For British Airways flights, for example, hovering over any date brings up a detailed summary of the number of award seats available in each cabin class on that date. SeatSpy will also show the number of Avios it would cost to book that seat through the British Airways Executive Club program.

SeatSpy availability calendar for Singapore-London flights on British Airways. Screenshot from SeatSpy website.

Using SeatSpy to find Etihad Airways award availability is a bit more tricky because the tool picks up both GuestSeat awards (which are available to book via partner airlines) and other types of awards which are only available to Etihad Guest members. AFF's article on SeatSpy explains how to look for Etihad availability using SeatSpy.

This pricing indicates Etihad GuestSeat availability. Screenshot from SeatSpy website.

If you're a Premium member, you can set up to four award seat alerts at a time.

SeatSpy lets you create award seat alerts.

This tool also offers integration with your AwardWallet account.

The availability on some airlines may not always exactly match what you can book using the points or miles of every one of that airline's partners. But as a starting point, SeatSpy can give you an excellent indication of what award availability might be out there.

## Subscription options

Without a SeatSpy account, you’ll be able to conduct a small number of free searches. In the results, you’ll see which dates have availability in Economy Class and how many seats are available each month in premium cabins.

You can create an account and upgrade to a monthly or annual subscription to get unlimited searches, view award availability for each day across all cabin classes, and access other features such as alerts. A 14-day free trial is available.

Subscription prices are in British Pounds (GBP). A "Premium" subscription costs £2.99/month (~AU$6), while "First Class" subscriptions cost £7.99/month (~AU$15). If you purchase an annual subscription, you’ll receive two months for free each year.

For most people, a Premium subscription would be adequate.
