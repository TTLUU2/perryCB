# Car Hire Loyalty Programs

**Car Hire Loyalty Programs**

Save time and earn freebies by taking advantage of car hire loyalty programs.

In this training unit, we will cover…

## Why car hire loyalty programs?

If you're reading this, you're probably a member of at least one frequent flyer program. You may have also joined some hotel and supermarket loyalty programs. But what about car rental programs?

Car hire loyalty programs are often overlooked. But if you ever rent cars, it's worth signing up. Here's why…

## Save time and skip the queues

Members have access to the priority queue. In some cases there is no need to even go to the counter at all. You'll also be able to save your preferences in your profile, meaning that the process of picking up your car will be quicker and hassle-free. Anyone who is a member can benefit from these time savings, even if you don't have status.

## Member discounts

Many programs also offer special discounts to its members. These discounts aren't just on hire cars – one program even offers discounts on stays at Accor hotels! These discounts are often available to all members, even if you've only just joined.

## Free rentals & upgrades

These are some of the benefits available if you hire cars frequently enough to earn status with a car hire loyalty program. Just like airline programs, the car hire loyalty programs have different status tiers designed to recognise loyal customers. The benefits of each status tier vary by program, but common benefits include free vehicle upgrades, bonus points, a free additional driver and free car hires.

Signing up is free, and only takes a few minutes to do online. So you really don't have anything to lose by signing up to the loyalty program whenever you rent a car!

## Hertz Gold Plus Rewards

## Earning and redeeming points

With Hertz you can **choose between earning airline frequent flyer points or Hertz Gold Plus Rewards points for each rental**.

Hertz partners with an impressive range of airlines, including Virgin Australia, Singapore Airlines, American Airlines, Alaska Airlines, United, and many other useful airline programs. However, Qantas is one airline that's *not* on the list. Qantas points can only be earned for hiring a car with Avis or Budget.

**If you opt to earn Hertz Gold Plus Rewards points, these can be redeemed for free rentals.** There are various options for daily, weekly, weekend and speciality vehicle rewards. See the Hertz website for current reward rates.

Generally we recommend crediting your points to an airline. There are a few reasons for this:

- You would need to earn quite a lot of points for a free car rental

- Lower-priced "Standard" rewards are subject to limited availability

- Reward bookings do not include taxes, insurance, premium location fees or any other charges which must be paid using real money (this is common to most car hire loyalty programs - not just Hertz)

- Hertz Gold Plus Rewards points expire if your account is inactive for 12 months and could change its expiration policy at any time.

## Hertz status levels

**Gold Plus Rewards** (base level):

- Expedited vehicle pick-up and drop-off

- Member-only discounts

- No additional driver fees

- Earn Hertz Gold Plus Rewards points **or **airline miles

**Five Star** (equivalent to Velocity Gold):

- All Gold Plus Rewards benefits

- Free one-class vehicle upgrades (subject to availability)

- 25% bonus points

- **To qualify: 10 rentals or USD2,000 spend within 12 months**

**President's Circle** (equivalent to Velocity Platinum):

- All Five Star benefits

- Free one-class upgrades (guaranteed)

- 50% bonus points

- Guaranteed vehicle availability

- Dedicated Hertz Rewards line

- **To qualify: 15 rentals or USD3,000 spend within 12 months**

## Europcar Privilege

## Earning and redeeming points

The Europcar Privilege program is a little bit different. **Instead of rewarding customers with points, it offers "rewards". These are earned as you hire more vehicles and climb up the status ladder.**

You can also choose to earn frequent flyer or hotel loyalty points whenever you hire a car. These are in addition to your Europcar Privilege benefits.

Europcar partners with a range of airline and hotel programs, including Virgin Australia. Customers can earn 4 Velocity points per dollar spent on car rentals within Australia, and overseas rentals earn a flat 800 Velocity points per hire.

Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer, Emirates Skywards & Qatar Airways Privilege Club are among other program partners. See the Europcar website for a full list of other Europcar Privilege partners.

## Europcar status levels

**Privilege Club** (base level):

- Express Service

- Dedicated line at pick-up counters

- Guaranteed car availability

- Discounts on some car hires

- Up to 15% off Accor hotel bookings

- $15 coupon after 2nd rental

- Free weekend after 3rd rental

- Earn frequent flyer points with each car hire

**Privilege Executive** (equivalent to Velocity Gold):

- All Privilege Club benefits

- Free vehicle upgrades

- $30 coupon after 5th rental

- **To qualify: 10 rentals or 40 rental days within 24 months**

**Privilege Elite** (equivalent to Velocity Platinum):

- All Privilege Executive benefits

- One free weekend rental per year

- Free additional driver

- $45 coupon after 5th rental

- **To qualify: 25 rentals or 85 rental days within 24 months**

**Privilege Elite VIP**:

- All Privilege Elite benefits

- Free double upgrade

- Free Standard Priority Pass membership (including 1 free lounge visit)

- $45 coupon after 4th rental

- **To qualify: 40 rentals or 130 rental days within 24 months**

Based on our research, this seems to be one of the better car hire programs out there. If you're hiring from Europcar regularly, you've got nothing to lose by signing up. You'll get discounted car rentals, as well as free upgrades and hires as you move up each status level.

Plus, status is valid for two years.

## Other car hire loyalty programs

Many of the other car hire companies have their own loyalty programs. These include:

- Avis Preferred

- Budget Fastbreak

- Thrifty Blue Chip

- Sixt Cards

- Enterprise Plus

If you ever rent a car with any of these companies, it's worth signing up just to avoid the queues when picking up your car! However, you should compare the value of earning points within these programs versus transferring them to an airline loyalty program.

## Earning frequent flyer points for car rentals

If you'd prefer to earn Qantas or Velocity frequent flyer points for your car rentals, there are plenty of options.

## Earning Qantas Frequent Flyer points

- Earn points with Avis & Budget

- 4 Qantas points per dollar on rentals within Australia

- Additional 4 Qantas Business Rewards points per dollar (within Australia)

- 700 Qantas points per hire overseas

## Earning Velocity Frequent Flyer points

- Earn points with Europcar, Hertz, Thrifty, Dollar Car Rental, Ace Rental Cars and SIXT

- 800 points per hire overseas

## Other frequent flyer programs

Many car rental companies will also give you the option to earn points with many other frequent flyer programs and/or hotel loyalty programs. So car rentals could also be a good way to earn points with more obscure overseas programs for which points are harder to earn in Australia.

If earning a fixed number of points per hire, check whether this is a fixed amount per day or for the full period. This can make a big difference to your points haul if you're renting a car for a week or more!

Where points are earned on a "per dollar" basis, check whether points are earned on the taxes, insurance and other additional payments - or only on the base price. Anyone who has hired a car will know that the base rate often makes up only a fraction of the total price, so this is an important consideration.
