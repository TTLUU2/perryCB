# Frequent Flyer Programs to Join Now

**Frequent Flyer Programs to Join Now**

Take a moment now to decide which frequent flyer programs might be useful to you. Join these now so you can be prepared and start taking advantage of special deals!

In this training unit, we will cover...

## Why you should join the featured frequent flyer programs now

We've already introduced you to several frequent flyer programs that we would recommend joining. Over the rest of the training, we'll feature even more.

Take a few minutes now to think about which frequent flyer programs you might want to use. Once you've chosen some (or all!) programs, you should join each of them now.

There are a few reasons that we recommend joining now:

- New members are often excluded from promotions

- Start earning points straight away

Almost all frequent flyer programs are free to join and it takes just a few minutes online. So **you have nothing to lose by signing up now**!

We would also recommend signing up the rest of your family to some or all of these programs. This enables you to earn and/or buy even more points and increases the chances of receiving valuable targeted offers.

## Some frequent flyer programs exclude residents of certain countries

The vast majority of loyalty programs welcome members from anywhere in the world. But Velocity Frequent Flyer only technically allows residents of the following countries to join its program:

- Australia

- New Zealand

- Papua New Guinea

- Fiji

- Cook Islands

- Vanuatu

- Tonga

- Solomon Islands

- Samoa

Virgin Australia only technically allows residents of certain countries to join its Velocity Frequent Flyer program. Photo: Virgin Australia.

British Airways Executive Club no longer prevents Australian & New Zealand residents from joining. That rule was a hangover from the years when British Airways had a joint venture with Qantas and was finally removed in 2021.
