# Etihad Guest

**Etihad Guest**

Purchase Etihad Guest miles for to redeem on a wide selection of partner airlines including Garuda Indonesia.

In this training unit, we will cover...

## Why buy Etihad Guest miles?

Etihad Guest sells miles relatively cheaply, with regular bonus-mile offers also available.

Etihad has around 20 partner airlines. These include Air Serbia, Hainan Airlines and many other airlines that aren't otherwise part of an alliance or easily accessible through Australian frequent flyer programs. You can redeem Etihad Guest miles to fly with any of these partner airlines.

Etihad introduced some incredibly punitive changes to award bookings in 2024. These include harsh cancellation fees, with the minimum being 25% for cancelling over 21 days in advance - some of the harshest in the industry. So make sure you're committed to booking!

Photo: Etihad Airways.

## Buying Etihad Guest miles

Etihad Guest miles cost USD20 per thousand at full price.

You can buy up to 100,000 miles per year, or 150,000 miles if you have Etihad Guest Platinum status. Miles should post to your account within 24-48 hours.

Any Etihad Guest member can buy miles. From time to time, Etihad offers a modest amount of bonus miles, with up to 60% bonuses available. You can buy miles on the Etihad Guest website.

If you're planning to redeem your miles for Etihad Airways flights, you only need to purchase 75% of the required miles. Up to 25% of the required miles for an Etihad redemption can be purchased at the time of booking at a rate *lower *than 2c per mile. However, this option is only available for Etihad-operated flights.

## How to book

You can book award flights operated by many partner airlines, but not all, on the Etihad website.

When booking Etihad flights, GuestSeat awards are the best value.

Etihad Airways awards can be booked on the Etihad website. Look for "GuestSeat" award availability, as this is the best value.

**To book award flights on partner airlines that are unavailable online, you'll need to call Etihad Guest.** From Australia, the phone number is 1300 532 215. Partner airline award flights must be booked at least 24 hours in advance.

If you find that the call centre agent you're speaking to is not familiar with the process of booking partner airline awards, try hanging up and calling again. For what it's worth, we've generally found that the agents in Etihad's Belgrade call centre to be the most helpful. If you're in Australia, you'll be more likely to reach this call centre when calling in the late evening or early morning.

## Award routing rules

- Awards are charged on a per-sector basis. Where there are no direct flights, connecting flights are charged as two (or more) separate flights.

- You cannot use multiple airlines on a single award ticket.

## Award booking fees

There are no booking fees, except for bookings made over the phone on airlines that are available to book online.

You can change the date on your ticket up to 21 days before departure for a fee of AED100 (~AU$42) per person.

Award bookings attract the following cancellation fees, depending on how far in advance you cancel:

- More than 21 days before departure: 25% penalty

- 8-21 days before departure: 50% penalty

- 1-7 days before departure: 75% penalty

- Within 24 hours before departure: 100% penalty

These are among the highest award cancellation fees of any frequent flyer program.

## Summary: Buying Etihad Guest miles

- Etihad Guest miles cost USD20 per thousand

- Etihad offers 20-60% bonus miles in promotions a few times per year

- Etihad Guest miles can be redeemed on around 20 partner airlines including Garuda Indonesia

- Miles are best used for short-haul flights, but some long-haul awards can also be good value
