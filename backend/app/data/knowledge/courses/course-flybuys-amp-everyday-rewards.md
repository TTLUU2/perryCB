# Flybuys &amp; Everyday Rewards

**Flybuys & Everyday Rewards**

Maximise your Flybuys points, Everyday Rewards points and supermarket savings! This unit contains an overview of earning and redeeming points at Coles and Woolworths.

In this training unit, we will cover…

## Earning Flybuys points

Flybuys is the loyalty program of Coles. Naturally, you can earn points by shopping at Coles... but there are many other ways to earn points too.

You can earn points with any Flybuys partner. These include:

You can also earn Flybuys points with a Coles Rewards Mastercard (2 points per $1 spent), or by transferring credit card reward points from NAB Rewards, Citi Rewards or Suncorp Rewards.

You'll earn 1 Flybuys base point per $1 spent at Coles and most other partners. Some retailers award points at a higher rate. You can also earn bonus points at Coles if you have a Coles insurance policy.

Bonus Flybuys point bundles with Coles insurance policies

**Flybuys points expire (and your account will be closed) if you don't earn or redeem at least one point every 12 months**. This shouldn't be a problem if you shop every now and again at Coles, but beware that this isn't a long time.

**You can also earn Virgin Australia Velocity status credits at Coles**! By linking your Flybuys and Velocity accounts, you'll earn 1 Velocity status credit for every $100 spent at Coles, up to a maximum of 10 status credits per month.

Considering that you need to earn 250 status credits just to earn Velocity Silver status, you won't earn anything meaningful from shopping at Coles alone. But, this could be a convenient way to top up an existing status credit balance.

## Transferring Flybuys points to Velocity Frequent Flyer

1,000 Flybuys points can be converted to 500 Velocity points (i.e. at a 2:1 conversion rate). You can do this on the Flybuys website.

Transfer Flybuys points to Velocity points on the Flybuys website.

**At a 2:1 conversion rate, you're effectively paying 1 cent per Velocity point.** Is this better value than redeeming Flybuys points for gift vouchers? It depends!

As you can see, redemptions on the ground for things like gift cards give you less than 1 cent per point of value. But redeeming for Reward Seats and upgrades gives you greater value. **So, it's only better value to transfer points to Velocity if you want to redeem for flights or upgrades!**

Keep an eye out for the regular offers on transferring points from Flybuys to Velocity. These could include bonus points or competitions. You may get a higher bonus or more competition entries if you've opted in to Velocity Auto Transfer.

## Earning Everyday Rewards points

Everyday Rewards (formerly Woolworths Rewards) is the other major supermarket loyalty program in Australia. Everyday Rewards is extremely similar to Flybuys, but there are fewer overall ways to earn and redeem points.

You can earn 1 Everyday Rewards point per $1 spent at stores including:

- Woolworths & Woolworths Metro

- Big W

- BWS

- Caltex & Caltex-Woolworths fuel outlets

- Ampol

- SuperPharmacy

Everyday Rewards is the loyalty program of major retailers including Woolworths. Photo: Matt Graham.

**Everyday Rewards points will expire if your account is inactive for 18 months.**

## Redeeming Everyday Rewards points

There are only two ways to redeem Everyday Rewards points:

- 2,000 points = $10 off a Woolworths shop

- 2,000 points = 1,000 Qantas Frequent Flyer points

**If you prefer to earn Qantas points, you'll need to opt-in on the Everyday Rewards website.** Each time you reach 2,000 Everyday Rewards points, they'll be converted to 1,000 Qantas points and arrive in your Qantas Frequent Flyer account within 24 hours.

By converting to Qantas points, you're effectively paying 1 cent per frequent flyer point. This is only worthwhile if you're redeeming your Qantas points for reward flights or upgrades where you'll get greater value than one cent per point.

## Banking your Everyday Rewards points for Christmas

You can also choose to "bank" your Everyday Rewards points for Christmas. If you select this option, the points will sit in your Everyday Rewards account until you either redeem them at Woolworths in December, or change your selection to Qantas points.

If you change your reward selection from "bank for Christmas" to "Qantas points", any points stored in your Everyday Rewards account will convert to Qantas points within 24 hours. (This could be a good way to "store" the points outside of your Qantas Frequent Flyer account if you want to control the timing of the points reaching your Qantas account, e.g. for Points Club qualification.)

## Flybuys vs Everyday Rewards

With both Flybuys and Everyday Rewards, 2,000 points are worth 1,000 Velocity or Qantas points, respectively. However, in our opinion, **Flybuys offers a more competitive overall program** for several reasons:

- Flybuys offers more ways to redeem points

- The program offers many more opportunities to earn points at retailers other than Coles

- With Flybuys, you can earn Velocity status credits for shopping at Coles

- Flybuys tends to send out much better and more frequent targeted bonus offers. (As you'll learn in the next unit, the true value in supermarket loyalty programs lies in these offers!)
