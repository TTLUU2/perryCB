# Complete Overview of Credit Card Reward Programs

**Complete Overview of Credit Card Reward Programs** This unit compares the major credit card reward programs in the Australian market. In this training unit, we will cover... ## Amex Membership Rewards **Amex Membership Rewards is one of the best value credit card rewards program in Australia. If you're serious about collecting frequent flyer points, an Amex credit card will be a great help. Amex Membership Rewards points are valuable because they can be transferred to 11-12 different airline frequent flyer programs and 2 hotel loyalty programs. This gives you the flexibility to transfer the points as required to airlines across different alliances. Most transfers take place at a 2:1 ratio. You can access even more frequent flyer programs by first transferring points to Marriott Bonvoy, although the conversion rate isn't quite as good. We'll cover this strategy in more detail in later courses. Amex Membership Rewards points do not expire, as long as your account is active and you pay your credit card bills on time. **Direct redemption options:** - Gift cards (0.5 CPP in value)* - David Jones (0.5 CPP in value) - Amex Travel Online (0.5 CPP in value) - Helloworld travel gift cards (0.5 CPP in value) - Cash back (0.5 CPP in value) *CPP = Cents Per Point **Transfer partners:** There are various different variations of the Amex Membership Rewards program in Australia. Your program will depend on your credit card. This matters because there are some slight differences in transfer partners available, depending on the Amex Membership Rewards program you're enrolled in: - **Qantas Frequent Flyer** transfers are **only** available to Ascent Premium members (i.e. Amex Platinum and Amex Centurion cardholders) Note that Amex transfer rates and partners changed on 15 December 2025.
