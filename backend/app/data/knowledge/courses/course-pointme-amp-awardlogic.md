# Point.me &amp; AwardLogic

**Point.me & AwardLogic**

Search for real-time award availability across multiple airlines with Point.me or AwardLogic.

In this training unit, we will cover...

## Finding award availability with Point.me

Point.me (formerly Juicy Miles) is a modern award availability search tool. The website is very easy to use and searches across many different airlines, alliances and loyalty programs simultaneously, and even has an explore mode.

Screenshot of Point.me explore mode

Award availability is shown in real-time. The main downside is that some of the options shown may not actually be accessible to you. The tool searches for options that are available to American credit card holders; some flights may not necessarily be available to most Australians.

The tool might also show mixed-class award options, which may or may not be desirable. Also note that the tool does sometimes miss options that are available, so use it only in conjunction with other award availability searching tools and methods.

Nonetheless, it is likely to find you something useful. If it would be necessary to transfer points to book the award, instructions are provided.

You can also link your Point.me account with your AwardWallet account.

## Subscription options

Point.me uses a subscription-based model. You can buy an annual subscription to get a year's worth of unlimited searches for USD129, or pay USD12 per month.

Point.me is a user-friendly and fairly powerful tool, so in our view this fee could be worthwhile if you're doing very frequent award searches. You just need to filter through the results that require you to use overseas frequent flyer programs and/or transfer points from a US credit card rewards program.

## Finding award availability with AwardLogic

AwardLogic works similarly to Point.me, but may be more useful for Australians because it specifically supports Australian loyalty programs.

You can switch between the US, UK or Australian markets. If you select to view results for the Australian market, you'll be able to view real-time award flight options that are available to book through loyalty programs including:

- CommBank Awards

- ANZ Rewards

- Everyday Rewards (transferrable to Qantas Frequent Flyer)

- Amex Membership Rewards

- Amplify Rewards (the loyalty program of St.George, Bank SA and Bank of Melbourne)

- HSBC Rewards Plus

- NAB Rewards

- Suncorp Bank Rewards

- BOQ Q Rewards

- Flybuys (transferrable to Velocity Frequent Flyer)

For example, if you have ANZ Rewards points, you can filter the options so that it only shows flights that you'd be able to book by transferring ANZ Rewards points to one of its partner airline programs (Velocity, Cathay, KrisFlyer or Airpoints).

Of course, you can also filter by airline programs (such as Qantas Frequent Flyer, Velocity KrisFlyer, United MileagePlus, Air Canada Aeroplan, and so on).

An example of an AwardLogic search for Business Class award seats from Sydney to Singapore.

With AwardLogic, you need to search for one route and cabin class at a time. When searching, you can choose to also show flights up to 1 or 2 days either side of your preferred travel date.

## Subscription options

AwardLogic offers three different subscription lengths:

- USD4.99 for a single-day pass

- USD19.99/month for a monthly subscription with unlimited searches

- USD199.99/year for an annual plan

You can also get a 3-day free trial on the monthly and annual plans.
