# Virgin Australia Status

**Virgin Australia Status**

Velocity Frequent Flyer status is generally a bit easier to earn than Qantas status. It comes with lots of useful benefits when you fly with Virgin Australia.

In this training unit, we will cover...

## Velocity status tiers

Virgin Australia's Velocity Frequent Flyer program offers three status tiers: Silver, Gold and Platinum.

There are lots of useful benefits for Velocity status holders when flying with Virgin Australia, including several benefits that Qantas does not offer. For example, Platinum members receive four annual complimentary Business Class upgrades and free extra-legroom seating.

However, Virgin Australia is not part of a global airline alliance and has a smaller network of international partner airlines than Qantas. Furthermore, the reciprocal status benefits given to Velocity members by Virgin's partner airlines are a bit hit-and-miss. For example, some partner airlines don't provide lounge access to Velocity Gold or Platinum members, or only provide limited access at certain airports.

## Velocity status benefits on Virgin Australia

Here's an overview of the key status benefits when flying Virgin Australia...

**Velocity Silver:**

- 2x complimentary Virgin Australia Lounge passes

- Additional baggage allowance

- 50% bonus points

**Velocity Gold:**

- Lounge access with Virgin Australia & selected international partner airlines + 1 guest

- Lounge access at the arrival airport for up to one hour after arriving

- Priority check-in, baggage and boarding

- Fly Ahead (on Economy Flex, Business fares & Reward Seats)

- 75% bonus points

**Velocity Platinum:**

- Complimentary Economy X (extra legroom) seats & free seat selection on Economy Lite tickets

- 4x free annual upgrades from Economy Flex to Business

- Up to 3 lounge guests

- Fly Ahead (on all fares except Economy Lite)

- Free on-board Wi-Fi (where available)

- 100% bonus points

Platinum members get complimentary access to Virgin Australia Economy X seats. Photo: Virgin Australia.

Note that access is only available to Virgin Australia Lounges when flying with Virgin Australia. These are all in Australian domestic terminals, so no lounge access is provided when flying Virgin internationally. An exception is the VA wet-lease flights to Doha, which do provide lounge access for both status members and those flying Business Class.

A full list of Velocity status benefits is available on the Velocity website.

## Velocity status qualification requirements

To earn Velocity status, you'd need to earn a minimum number of status credits and complete sufficient eligible flight sectors within any rolling 12-month period. Unlike Qantas, there are **no fixed membership years**.

You'll be upgraded as soon as your past 12 months of status activity is above the threshold.

Once you upgrade to a higher status tier, your status will be valid for 12 months. You'll be allocated a "review date" which is a year away - to renew your status for a further year, you'd need to meet that renewal criteria within those next 12 months.

Eligible Virgin Australia sectors, for the purpose of earning status, are Virgin Australia marketed flights in Economy Choice, Economy Flex or Business. Economy Lite fares, Reward Seats and flights marketed by partner airlines do not count as eligible Velocity sectors towards status.

Virgin Australia will offer a lifetime Gold status, called Forever Gold, from 1 October 2025.

## Earning status credits

Velocity status credits can be earned by flying on Virgin Australia and most partner airlines. For Virgin Australia flights, the number of status credits you'll earn for any given flight depends on the cost of the ticket. You’ll earn 1 status credit per $12 spent on most Virgin Australia Choice, Flex and Business fares. This is **rounded up** to the nearest $12.

A lower earn rate of 1 status credit per $24 applies to Economy Lite bookings.

Different earn rates apply to Virgin Australia flights as part of an international partner itinerary, and Virgin Australia's flights to Doha. See the Velocity website for other status credit earn tables.

A different status credit earning table also applies when flying with partner airlines.

You can see the specific status credit earn tables for each partner airline on the Velocity website, but you'll generally earn Velocity status credits at the following rates when flying with partner airlines:

## Earning Velocity status credits without flying

There are also a few ways to earn Velocity status credits without flying.

One of the main methods is shopping at Coles, Liquorland or First Choice Liquor. You can earn 1 status credit for every $100 spent at Coles, up to 10 status credits per month, by linking your Flybuys and Velocity accounts.

It would be impossible to gain any meaningful status purely from Coles shopping, but this can be a handy way to supplement the status credits earned from flying.

Link your Flybuys and Velocity Frequent Flyer accounts to earn status credits when shopping at Coles.

You can also earn an extra 100 Velocity status credits by spending at least $50,000 on an American Express Velocity Platinum credit card each year. Plus, when paying for Virgin Australia flights using a Virgin Money Velocity High Flyer card, you can earn 20% bonus status credits (up to 100 per year).

## Family Pooling

Family Pooling is a unique benefit of Velocity Frequent Flyer.

Virgin allows up to 2 adults and 4 children (under 18) living at the same address to pool their Velocity points & status credits into a single account.

This is a great benefit for families that travel together as it allows a parent to earn status much more quickly!

And if you're travelling as a family, only one member of the family needs status for everyone to enjoy the benefits. That's because Gold members are entitled to 1 guest and up to 2 additional children under 12 at Virgin's lounges. Platinum members get 3 complimentary guests (excluding children).

Once a family points pool is set up, a nominated adult becomes the "beneficiary" and will receive the points & status credits earned by all "contributors".

Another great Velocity Frequent Flyer benefit for Gold and Platinum members is guaranteed reward seat availability for a family holiday. This benefit is available for travel in Economy Class once per year. Bookings must be made at least six months in advance.
