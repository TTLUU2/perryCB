# Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer Program Overview

KrisFlyer is the frequent flyer program of Singapore Airlines, widely regarded as one of the world's best carriers. For Australians, it is a particularly attractive program thanks to Singapore Airlines' extensive Australian network and its position within the Star Alliance.

## Star Alliance Access

As a Star Alliance member, KrisFlyer miles can be redeemed on over 25 partner airlines including United Airlines, Lufthansa, ANA, Thai Airways, Air New Zealand, and EVA Air. This opens up a vast network of routes globally.

## Earning KrisFlyer Miles from Australia

- **Credit card transfers:** This is the primary way Australians build a KrisFlyer balance. American Express Membership Rewards, Citi Rewards, and several other bank programs transfer directly to KrisFlyer.
- **Flying:** Earn miles on Singapore Airlines, SilkAir, Scoot, and Star Alliance partner flights departing Australia.
- **KrisShop and partners:** Earn through Singapore Airlines' online retail portal and select partners.

## Redemption Highlights

KrisFlyer is best known for unlocking Singapore Airlines' own premium cabin products:

- **Business class (SYD/MEL/BNE to Singapore):** Approximately 62,000 miles one-way — a strong sweet spot for one of the best business class seats in the sky.
- **Suites and first class:** Singapore Airlines' iconic Suites product on the A380 can be booked with KrisFlyer miles, though availability is limited.
- **Star Alliance partners:** Use miles for flights on ANA, Thai Airways, Lufthansa, and others when Singapore Airlines does not fly the route you need.

## Key Considerations

KrisFlyer miles expire after 36 months, so you need to plan your earning and burning cycle accordingly. Transferring credit card points just before booking is a common strategy to avoid expiry. The program also offers an online waitlist feature for award seats, which can be handy for popular routes.

For Australians chasing premium cabin experiences, KrisFlyer is an essential program to have in your toolkit.
