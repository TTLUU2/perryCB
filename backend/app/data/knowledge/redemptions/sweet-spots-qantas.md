# Best Value Qantas Points Redemptions

Not all Qantas Points redemptions are created equal. The sweet spots below offer significantly more value per point than average, making them the best ways to use your Qantas balance.

## Domestic Economy

Short-haul domestic economy flights start from just **8,000 points** one-way plus taxes. Routes like Sydney to Melbourne or Brisbane to Sydney are among the most accessible redemptions and offer solid value, especially when cash fares are high during peak periods.

## Sydney to Fiji — Business Class

At **36,000 points** one-way in Fiji Airways business class, this is one of the best-value international business class redemptions in the Qantas program. The flight is under four hours, and you get a lie-flat seat, lounge access, and premium service.

## Japan Airlines Business Class to Tokyo

Booking JAL business class from the Australian east coast to Tokyo costs approximately **72,000 points** one-way. JAL's business class product is consistently rated among the best in the world, featuring the Sky Suite III seat with direct aisle access. Availability can be found with patience, particularly in shoulder seasons.

## Emirates Business and First Class

Thanks to the Qantas-Emirates partnership, you can redeem Qantas Points on Emirates flights. Business class to Dubai starts around **72,000 points** one-way, giving you access to Emirates' A380 product with the onboard bar and lounge. First class — with the iconic onboard shower — is available from approximately **108,000 points** one-way.

## Cathay Pacific Business Class to Hong Kong

Redeem through oneworld for Cathay Pacific business class from Australia to Hong Kong at around **60,000 points** one-way. Cathay's business class is excellent, and Hong Kong serves as a convenient gateway to the rest of Asia.

## Tips for Booking

- Search for Classic Flight Rewards on the Qantas website, which uses the fixed-rate award chart.
- Book as early as possible — Qantas releases reward seats up to 353 days in advance.
- Use Point Hacks Seat Alerts to monitor hard-to-find routes.
