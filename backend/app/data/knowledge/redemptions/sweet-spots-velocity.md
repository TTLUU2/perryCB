# Best Value Velocity Points Redemptions

Velocity Points can unlock exceptional value when used on the right routes. Here are the standout sweet spots that deliver the most bang for your points.

## Domestic Short-Haul Business Class

Virgin Australia business class on short domestic routes starts from just **15,500 points** one-way plus taxes. For routes like Sydney to Melbourne or Brisbane to Sydney, this is outstanding value — you get lounge access, priority boarding, a comfortable seat, and food and drinks. Availability is generally good, making this one of the most reliable sweet spots in any Australian program.

## Singapore Airlines Business Class

Booking Singapore Airlines business class from Australia to Singapore costs **62,000 Velocity Points** one-way. Singapore Airlines consistently ranks among the world's best carriers, and their business class product features a lie-flat seat with direct aisle access on most aircraft. Availability from Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane is reasonable, particularly midweek.

## ANA Business Class to Tokyo

All Nippon Airways (ANA) business class from Australia to Tokyo via their partner network is a standout redemption. ANA's business class product — known as "The Room" on the 777 — is widely considered one of the best in the sky. Velocity pricing for this route offers strong value compared to the cash fare.

## Etihad Business Class to Abu Dhabi

Etihad's business class product is excellent, and redeeming Velocity Points for Etihad flights to Abu Dhabi (and connecting onwards to Europe or Africa) provides competitive value. The Etihad A380 business class with its onboard bar is a highlight.

## Tips for Booking

- Search reward availability on the Virgin Australia website under "Use Points" or call the Velocity member centre.
- Partner airline bookings (Singapore Airlines, ANA, Etihad) often require a phone call or online search through the dedicated partner reward section.
- Set up Point Hacks Seat Alerts for competitive routes to be notified when seats open up.
- Book early — partner reward seats are limited and popular routes sell out months in advance.
